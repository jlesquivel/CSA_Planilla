To Uninstall Planilla

EXAMPLE UNINSTALL

  1. Click the [Start] button.
  2. Select Settings > Control Panel.
  3. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.
  4. Click Install/Uninstall.
  5. Then select Planilla from the list of programs that can be automatically removed.
  6. Click the [Remove...] button to uninstall Planilla.
